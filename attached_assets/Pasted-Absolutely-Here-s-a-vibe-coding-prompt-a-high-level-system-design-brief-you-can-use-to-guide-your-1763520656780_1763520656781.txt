## Vibe Coding Prompt for Cycle Sync App

**Project Soul:**  
A neuro-affirming, relationship-strengthening cycle syncing app that transforms data into real daily support, deepens partner understanding, and makes self-care social, collaborative, and fun.

### Core Principles  
- **Personal, not clinical:** Use friendly, inclusive, non-medical language everywhere.  
- **Connection > Tracking:** The real magic is in conversation, rituals, and active partners—not just logging data.  
- **Neuro-friendly:** Rely on visual progress, reminders, streaks, audio tips, and "just one thing" daily nudges.
- **Customizable, not one-size-fits-all:** Let users pick what they see, what they share, and how they interact.

### Feature Requirements  
1. **Partner Support Playbook**  
   - Send phase-specific “how to support her today” cards/tips.  
   - Option for quick “care request” (user chooses from snack run, touch, chill, encouragement, etc.).  
   - Gamified or gratitude-based tracking for partner participation.

2. **Cycle-Integrated Shared Calendar**  
   - Both users sync schedules/events, color-coded by phase.  
   - Partner can see daily energy/mood forecasts and cycle stage at a glance (with user permission toggle).

3. **AI-Powered Suggestions**  
   - “Aunt B” answers for cycle, moods, and relationship Qs—all advice tailored by phase and personal health log.  
   - Dynamic supplement and recipe tips, based on user logs and tracked symptoms.  
   - Habit/routine tips personalized by cycle phase and energy/motivation level.

4. **Guidance Trackers**  
   - Phase-based habits (hydration, exercise, rest, etc.) and optional ADHD-friendly rituals.  
   - Celebratory streaks, badges, and gentle reminders when habits are kept up.

5. **Community Sourced Q&A**  
   - Anonymous, shareable partner Q&A knowledgebase: what helped in each phase, what didn’t, upvoted by community.

6. **Guided Ritual Library**  
   - Audio/video self-care practices (breathwork, meditation, journaling, movement) sorted by cycle phase.

7. **Doctor/Export Options**  
   - One-tap export of logs, symptom summaries, and mood tracking into shareable PDF for doctor visits.

8. **Inclusive Options**  
   - Onboarding flow offers non-binary, trans, poly, and solo configurations without gendered assumptions.

### Brand/UX Vibe  
- Whimsical but clear: soft colors, soothing fonts, gentle animations.  
- Empowering and shame-free: always frame symptoms, needs, and requests as normal and valid.  
- Micro-learning friendly: tips/cards are short, swipeable, “read this, try that” style.  
- Opt-in privacy: users control what’s shared with partners/others at all times.
